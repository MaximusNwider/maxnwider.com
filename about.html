About

Building from atoms to apps.

I design and build across RF/EM, embedded systems, and VLSI. My work style is analysis-first and measurement-driven: model it, prototype it, validate it, then document it so others can reproduce the results.

Recent projects include a wearable allergy smartwatch (sensors, BLE, 4-layer PCB), FinFET SRAM sense-amp studies (PTM-MG, HSPICE, Python automation), a corrugated horn with a matched feed network (HFSS + range measurements), and a small data-viz toolkit for lab pipelines.
